Logo Design Case Study

Panpacific University Student Council Logo

A logo design project created for the Panpacific University Student Council, focused on leadership, unity, hierarchy, and strong alignment with the university’s visual identity.

Logo Design Branding Visual Identity Concept Development

The Task

Create a professional and versatile logo for the Panpacific University Student Council that reflects leadership, unity, and school spirit while aligning with the university’s identity.

Design Goal

The goal was to create a recognizable mark that feels connected to Panpacific University while giving the Student Council its own clear and meaningful identity.

Concept Direction

The concept started with the university’s familiar delta-inspired structure and the idea of representing students through simplified people silhouettes. This allowed the logo to communicate unity, leadership, and hierarchy in a clean visual form.

Initial Task & Concept Notes

Visual Elements

  • Delta-inspired form based on the university identity
  • People silhouettes to represent students and unity
  • Hierarchy through ascending figure arrangement
  • Institutional color palette for consistency

Design Thinking

The design uses a group of figures arranged in a rising structure to symbolize student leadership, collective representation, and organized hierarchy within the council.

Process Development

Furnishing the Design

The final approach used silhouettes forming a delta shape, representing both the university’s identity and the leadership structure of the Student Council. The official university colors were applied to maintain brand consistency.

Refinement & Output

Final Output

The final logo was presented in both primary and horizontal formats, making it flexible for official documents, social media materials, event visuals, and organizational branding.

Outcome

  • Created a meaningful student council identity
  • Maintained alignment with university branding
  • Produced a versatile logo system
  • Showcased concept-to-creation design thinking

From Concept to Creation

This project demonstrates how visual research, symbolism, and institutional branding can come together to create a logo that is both meaningful and functional.
